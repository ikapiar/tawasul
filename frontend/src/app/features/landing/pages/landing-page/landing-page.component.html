About IKAPIAR

IKAPIAR (Ikatan Alumni Perguruan Islam Ar-Risalah) is the official alumni organization representing graduates of PIAR - Perguruan Islam Ar-Risalah, one of Indonesia's esteemed Islamic educational institutions.

Our mission is to maintain strong bonds among alumni, support our alma mater's development, and create a platform for mutual growth and collaboration. Through Tawasul, we bring our community together in the digital age, making it easier than ever to stay connected and engaged.
